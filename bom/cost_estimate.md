## Cost Optimization Strategies

1. **Bulk Discounts**: Contact Adafruit/DigiKey for 50+ unit discounts (typically 10-15% on Pico 2s and potentiometers)
2. **Shared Tools**: Instructors can share soldering irons and multimeters across multiple workshops
3. **USB Cables**: Buy 50-packs from Amazon (~$0.30-0.50 per cable) instead of individual cables
4. **Resistor/Capacitor Packs**: Buy bulk assortment packs rather than individual components
5. **Reusable Breadboards**: Students can keep breadboards; they're durable and reusable for future projects
