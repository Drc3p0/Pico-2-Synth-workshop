## Supplier Recommendations

### Primary Suppliers
- **Adafruit**: Best for educational bulk orders, excellent documentation, reliable shipping
- **DigiKey**: Competitive pricing on bulk resistors/capacitors, fast shipping
- **Sparkfun**: Good for Pico 2 and sensor breakouts, educational discounts available

### Bulk Order Tips
- Order Pico 2s and potentiometers in bulk (50+) for 10-15% discount
- Resistor/capacitor packs are cheaper than individual units
- USB-C cables: Consider bulk packs from Amazon (50-pack ~$0.30-0.50 per cable)
- Breadboards: Half-size breadboards in bulk packs are significantly cheaper

## Shipping & Handling

- **Standard shipping (5-7 days)**: ~$15-25 for bulk order
- **Expedited shipping (2-3 days)**: ~$40-60
- **Recommendation**: Order 2-3 weeks before workshop for standard shipping

## Instructor Overhead (Recommended)

For a 50-student workshop, budget for:
- **5-10 spare Pico 2s**: $25-50 (for defects/damage)
- **10 spare breadboards**: $20
- **Spare component assortment**: $30 (buttons, resistors, capacitors, wires)
- **Soldering iron + solder** (if doing optional RC filter): $30-50
- **USB hub/power supplies**: $20-30

**Total instructor overhead**: $125-180
